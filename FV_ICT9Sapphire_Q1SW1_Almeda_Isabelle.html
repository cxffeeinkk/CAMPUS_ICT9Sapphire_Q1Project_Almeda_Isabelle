Interesting Characters from Noli me Tangere

Juan Crisostomo Ibarra

Ibarra, being the book's main character, is a well-written and fascinating character to say the least.

Things to note of:

  • His travels to Europe for his studies
  • His relationships in the Philippines (Maria Clara, his father)
  • Being culturally different compared to others

    • Maria Clara

    Ibarra's love interest, she is a lovely character who makes appearances throughout the story.

    Things to note of:

  • It is said she was based off of Jose Rizal's real lover, Leonor Rivera.
  • She also portrays the likes of a young Filipina woman, sheltered during the Spanish occupation.
  • Maria Clara is considered the book's heroine.

    • Padre Damaso

    A very controversial character, many people dislike him- which is understandable, considering what he's done.

    Things to note of:

  • How he's portrayed as a friar, specifically
  • The message Jose Rizal wants to portray through his character
  • The fact that he threw a tantrum over tinola.

    • Kapitan Tiago

    A good man ruined by the likes of politics and colonisation, the reveal of his true nature was written betrayal.

    Things to note of:

  • He is well-liked by the general population and those in power due to money and reputation.
  • Kapitan Tiago looks down on Filipinos, despite being one himself.
  • His whole character's about power, and what one does to get it.

    • Don Rafael Ibarra

    Being the main character's father, his backstory and what lead to his demise is an intricate and fascinating thing to read.

    Things to note of:

  • He was a good man- he was minding his own business, though he made enemies as well.
  • He made sure to supply his son with a good life, and good studies.
  • Had he made it to Europe as well, he wouldn't have died.
